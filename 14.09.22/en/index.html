How to remove the background from a photo on iOS 16?

Each year, Apple brings more and more new features to devices. That is what happened this year as well. The ability to copy an object in a photo is not available for all iPhone models that support iOS 16. Here is the complete list of devices:

  • iPhone SE 2 generation.
  • iPhone SE 3 generation.
  • iPhone XR.
  • iPhone XS.
  • iPhone XS Max.
  • iPhone 11.
  • iPhone 11 Pro Max.
  • iPhone 12.
  • iPhone 12 Pro.
  • iPhone 12 Pro Max.
  • iPhone 13 mini.
  • iPhone 13.
  • iPhone 13 Pro.
  • iPhone 13 Pro Max.
  • iPhone 14.
  • iPhone 14 Plus.
  • iPhone 14 Pro.
  • iPhone 14 Pro Max.

To copy an object from an image or photo, you will need to do the following:

  1. Upload the picture you are interested in to the Photos app or take a photo.
  2. Go to Photos.
  3. Open the image.
  4. Hold your finger on the object you want to copy.
  5. As soon as the neural network determines its boundaries, it will be highlighted with a neat animation.
  6. In the context menu, click “Copy” or drag it using the Drag’N’Drop function to another application.

Such pictures can even be sent to friends in messengers!
